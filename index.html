Revenant.pk3

It's a revenant Doom mod! Most of the enemies are Revenants and so is the player! Your only weapons are your revenant cannons and your fists. All the ammo is now health bonuses, but all the map spawned weapons are upgrades for your cannons and fists. You can have up to 800 health with health bonuses, but only 400 with medikits and stimpacks.
